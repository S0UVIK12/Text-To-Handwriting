🤓 Guide to add your own handwriting

  1. Sign Up for Calligraphr

    • Visit the Calligraphr website: Go to Calligraphr and sign up for a free account.
    • Create your profile: Fill out the required information to create an account.

  2. Create a New Font Template

    • Log in to Calligraphr:After creating your account, log in to access your dashboard.
    • Download a template:Click on “Templates” in the left-hand menu and choose your alphabet (either English or other languages). You can choose either Basic (letters only) or Expanded (including numbers, punctuation, etc.). Calligraphr will generate a downloadable PDF template.

  3. Print the Template

    • Print the template:After downloading the PDF template, print it out on paper. This template will serve as the canvas for your handwriting.

  4. Write Your Handwriting on the Template

    • Fill out the template:Using a black pen, carefully write each letter, number, and symbol in the appropriate spaces. It’s best to use a felt-tip or gel pen for a consistent stroke.
    • Ensure neatness:Write neatly to ensure the digital version looks clean and professional.

  5. Scan the Filled Template

    • Scan the template:Once you’ve filled out the template with your handwriting, scan it at a resolution of at least 300 dpi to maintain the quality.
    • Save as an image:Save the scanned document as a PNG or JPEG file, as Calligraphr accepts these formats.

  6. Upload the Template Back to Calligraphr

    • Go back to Calligraphr:After scanning, return to the Calligraphr website.
    • Upload your scanned template:Click “My Fonts” and then “Upload Template.” Upload the scanned template file to the platform.
    • Convert to font:Calligraphr will process the image and generate the characters based on your handwriting. You can make adjustments if any characters need tweaking.

  7. Generate Your Handwriting Font

    • Preview the font:Calligraphr will allow you to preview your font, ensuring all letters look good. You can edit individual characters if necessary.
    • Download the font:Once you’re satisfied, click “Build Font” to generate your handwriting font. Download the file, which will typically be in TTF or OTF format.

  8. Upload Your Font to Textor

    • Visit our website,Textor:Now that you have your custom handwriting font, head over to Textor.
    • Upload your font:Follow the instructions on Textor for adding custom fonts. This might be through a special setting or file upload section where you can integrate your custom TTF/OTF font file.

  9. Customize and Test Your Handwriting

    • Apply the font:Select your newly added font from the Textor interface.
    • Test it out:Type a few sentences and see how your handwritten font is rendered on-screen. Make any necessary adjustments to size, color, or spacing to get the perfect handwritten look.

  10. Share and Enjoy!

    11. Now that you’ve successfully added your own handwriting, you can create personalized notes, invitations, and more using your authentic handwriting style. Share your custom handwritten creations with friends, family, or clients, and enjoy the personal touch you’ve added to your digital text!

❓ FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

1. What is Textor and how does it work?

Textor is an innovative tool that converts digital text into realistic handwriting. It mimics the appearance of handwritten notes by allowing users to select from various handwriting styles, adjust font size, and customize colors. Simply enter your text, choose the preferred handwriting style, and Textor generates a handwritten document that can be saved, shared, or printed. Check out Guide to add your own Handwriting

2. Can I add my own handwriting to Textor?

Yes! You can add your handwriting to Textor by creating a custom font through platforms like calligraphr. Once you've created and downloaded your font, you can upload it to Textor and use your unique handwriting for your text.

3. What formats can I export my handwritten text in?

You can export your text as a high-quality PDF or image file. This makes it easy to share digitally or print it out for physical distribution.

4. Can I use Textor to convert multiple pages of text?

Yes, Textor supports multi-page document generation. Whether you're creating a handwritten letter, report, or project, you can input longer text and export it as multiple pages in a PDF format.

5. Is the handwritten text customizable?

Absolutely! You can adjust the font size, color, and spacing to match your desired look. This flexibility helps you create handwritten text that feels personal and tailored to your preferences.
